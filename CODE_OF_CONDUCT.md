# Code of Conduct

BindKit uses a simple contributor standard:

- Be respectful and direct.
- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence.
- Do not harass, threaten, insult, dox, or discriminate.
- Keep security reports coordinated and private until disclosure is agreed.

Maintainers may hide, edit, or remove comments and contributions that violate
this standard. Repeated or severe violations may result in a ban from project
spaces.

Report conduct concerns to `security@bindfort.com`.
